About

S2 Sports was founded by Spencer Vincent. His vision was to create a place for athletes of all abilities to have access to top-of-the-line equipment. Vincent continues to research and expand his company to be used by athletes of all sports.

Goals

  • provide excellent equipment for student athletes of all abilities
  • give the community a place to exercise
  • allow students the opportunity to receive coaching from nation-known athletes
  • give back to the community
  • give access to top-of-the-line equipment

Athletic Camps Offered

We offer a variety of different sports camps to all levels of ability. Ranging from kindergarten age to adults, everyone will be able to find a camp that is right for them.
